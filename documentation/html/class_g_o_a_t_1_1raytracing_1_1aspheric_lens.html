Representation of aspheric lens The left and the right side is described by a formula \( z(r)=\frac{r^2}{R\left( 1+\sqrt{1-(1+\kappa)\frac{r^2}{R^2}}\right)}+\sum_l \alpha_l r^l \) or the side can also be plano.
